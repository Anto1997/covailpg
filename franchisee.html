FRANCHISEE

BUSINESS MODEL:
COVAI LPG Auto LPG Dispensing Station (ALDS) can be operated under the following models

1.Dealer Owned Dealer Operated (DODO)

The land is owned / leased by the Dealer and all the costs including the Land & Civil development and Equipment installation is borne by the Dealer and the ALDS is operated by the Dealer.

2.Dealer Owned Company Operated (DOCO)

The land is owned / leased by the Dealer and the cost for Land & Civil development alone is borne by the Dealer and the cost for Equipment installation is borne by the Company and the ALDS will be operated by the Company.

3.Company Owned Dealer Operated (CODO)

The land is owned / leased by the Company and the cost for Land & Civil development and Equipment installation is mutually borne by the Company & Dealer.

4.Company Owned Company Operated (COCO)

The land is owned / leased by the Company and all the costs including the Land & Civil development and Equipment installation is borne by the Company and the ALDS is operated by the Company.
