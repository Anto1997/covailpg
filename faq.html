Frequently Asked Questions

Can LPG be used legally as an Automotive fuel?

The use of LPG as an Automotive fuel has become legal in Indian with effect from April 24-2000 within the prescribed safety conditions.


How much time does it take to commission and make an ALDS fully operational?

Once all the required licenses are obtained from various departments, it takes 60 days to install and operate an ALDS.


What is source of Auto LPG in India?

Auto LPG is imported in India at various parts such as Holdia, Vizag, Pipawa, Tuticorin, Mumbai Etc. COVAI LPG has an agreement with M/s. SHV LPG India P.Ltd., Hyderabad for uninterrupted supply for Auto LPG.


I do not have a prior experience in handling petroleum products, will I be able to enter this business?

Yes you can operate such a business based on training and expertise offered by us.


Can I renew the license of business with the company if the current license expires?

Yes the license can be renewed again when it expires.
